Open workspace
Dashboard · design surfaces · v0.5

The workspace
our operators live in.

Fifteen screens plus five supporting surfaces. One spine. Every action a signed, sealed, countersigned event on an append-only chain. Editorial not because it's pretty — because prosecutors, judges, and defence clerks read from it under pressure, and clarity is a safety property.

15 screens · 5 supports · 5 clusters Shell: sidebar + topbar Chain: SHA-256 · Ed25519 · RFC 3161

④ Platform · the load-bearing floor
12 · Search

Semantic recall

Lexical + on-box embeddings. Queries never leave the instance and are themselves written to the audit log. ACL-respecting row-by-row.

dash-search.htmlOpen → 13 · Audit log

The chain

Warm off-black paper, dimmed accent, faster auto-lock, panic-lock always armed. Meant for a 3 a.m. disclosure review — not for looking good in a screenshot.

Shared shell

Sidebar with org switcher, signed-in operator and key icon, grouped navigation with live badges. Topbar carries breadcrumb, ⌘K search and notifications.

Design vocabulary

Fraunces for headings, Inter for text, JetBrains Mono for hashes and timestamps. Warm paper background, single accent, soft shadows. Editorial pacing, not SaaS.

Still open

Mobile-read mode for judges on the way to hearings. Touch-first ceremony screens for the HSM room. Accessibility audit. Internationalisation for at least FR, ES, UK.

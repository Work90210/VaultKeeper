# VaultKeeper

Sovereign evidence management platform for legal/criminal investigations.

## Design Context

See `.impeccable.md` for full design documentation.

**Brand**: Authoritative. Precise. Trustworthy.
**Direction**: Precision instrument with institutional weight — Bloomberg density meets Swiss restraint.
**Theme**: Light + dark (system preference). Light primary.
**Colors**: Warm stone neutrals, deep navy anchor, subdued amber/teal accent. No pure black/white.
**Typography**: Refined serif/geometric sans headings, legible sans body, monospace for codes only.
**Principles**: Density without clutter. Trust through precision. Typography-led hierarchy. Progressive disclosure. Calm authority.
